| ----------------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **ISA‚Ä¶IEA**                         | Handles file-level control (sender, receiver, date/time, etc.) | Ensures the file is legitimate and formatted correctly                                    |
| **NM1 (Submitter/Receiver)**        | Identifies who is sending/receiving the claim electronically   | Crucial for routing and data consistency                                                  |
